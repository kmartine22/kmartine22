Our Ocean

Facts about the Ocean!

The ocean is 3.8 billion years old and is very important to our Earth. The ocean produces about half the oxygen in our planet. It is very important to make sure our ocean is safe and clean. As well as the animals inside of it. Plastic and oil can really destroy our oceans and the ecosystems living inside of it. One way to really help reduce plastic pollution and overall pollution in our ocean is ssing reusable items. Using reusable items such as water bottles straws, bags instead of plastic bags and coffee mugs can help reduce the amount of plastic pollution. Simply picking up plastic when you see it on the ground or beach and throwing it away can also help our ecosystem and planet as a whole to become cleaner and healthier. These are different types of reusables you can use to help decrease plastic pollution and pollution overall. Metal straws, Coffee mugs, reusable bags, water bottles.
