The ocean is very important to us an humans and overall our planet! It is very important to help reduce plastic pollution or any type of pollution on our planet. Specifically plastic is very bad, it leads to harm of species and ecosystems in the ocean. If you want to help our planet, simply clean up or pick up any plastic or trash you see on the ground and throw it away! You can also click this button to donate to help stop and reduce pollution.

What does this link lead to?

This link leads to an orginization called 4ocean, they are an orginization that helps remove trash and plastic from the ocean. They are very cool because they sell products as well to help the cause! They sell these really cool bracelets dedicated to a type of species and get this! Each bracelet they make they use the plastic they find in the ocean to create them and reuse them!
