Ways to help the ocean and resolve pollution!.

You might be asking what is one way we can help get our oceans healthier again? One way to really help reduce plastic pollution and overall pollution in our ocean is using reusable items. Using reusable items such as water bottles straws, bags instead of plastic bags and coffee mugs can help reduce the amount of plastic pollution. Simply picking up plastic when you see it on the ground or beach and throwing it away can also help our ecosystem and planet as a whole to become cleaner and healthier.

These are different types of reusables you can use to help decrease plastic pollution and pollution overall. Metal straws, Coffee mugs, reusable bags, water bottles.
